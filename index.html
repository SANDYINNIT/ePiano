About Us
We are students from PUIG CASTELLAR, excited to present our project that we've been diligently working on. Our venture involves the creation of a company centered around our innovative product: the "ePiano" – an Electric Piano designed to revolutionize the music industry.
To bring our vision to life, we combined essential components with Arduino technology and plenty of programming. Meet our team: I'm Saihajdeep, and my partner is Alejandro. I focused on building our website, while Alejandro handled the company's documentation.
The idea behind the ePiano is simple yet powerful: to offer a piano experience that's hassle-free and versatile. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced musician, the ePiano promises to enhance your musical journey.
Join us as we embark on this exciting adventure with our company, "ePiano."
Thank you for your support!
